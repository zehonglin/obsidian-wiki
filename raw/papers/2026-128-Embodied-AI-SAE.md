## 摘要

Embodied AI is rapidly moving from research into real-world systems such as autonomous vehicles, mobile robots, and industrial machines. As these systems become more capable of perceiving, deciding, and acting in dynamic environments, they also introduce new challenges in safety, trust, governance, and operational reliability.

This white paper summarizes key insights from the SAE World Congress 2026 panel session "Embodied AI in Action", bringing together experts from automotive, robotics, AI, and safety engineering.

## 核心议题

1. **系统工程视角** — 具身 AI 是系统工程挑战，需要工程严谨性
2. **生命周期治理** — 从设计到部署到退役的全生命周期管理
3. **安全与信任** — 功能安全、预期功能安全（SOTIF）、可信 AI
4. **人因设计** — 人机协作中的人类中心设计原则
5. **标准演进** — ISO/SAE 标准的持续发展

## 核心观点

> "Long-term success will depend not only on advances in AI capability, but equally on safe and trustworthy deployment."
