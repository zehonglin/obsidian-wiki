## 摘要

Embodied AI is widely recognized as a cornerstone of AGI. Building on the success of LLMs and VLMs, VLA models have emerged to address language-conditioned robotic tasks by generating actions. This survey provides a detailed taxonomy of VLAs organized into three major lines of research:

1. **Individual components of VLAs** — 感知、语言理解、动作生成各模块
2. **VLA-based control policies** — 低层动作预测策略
3. **High-level task planners** — 长时程任务分解为子任务序列
