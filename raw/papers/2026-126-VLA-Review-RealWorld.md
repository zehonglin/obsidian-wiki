## 摘要

VLA models have gained significant attention for leveraging LLM/VLM advances in robotics. By unifying vision, language, and action data at scale, VLA models aim to learn policies that generalise across diverse tasks, objects, embodiments, and environments.

This systematic review covers:
1. **Strategy and architectural transition** — 从分离模型到统一架构的演进
2. **Architectures and building blocks** — 骨干网络、动作生成模块设计
3. **Modality-specific processing techniques** — 视觉/语言/动作各模态处理
4. **Learning paradigms** — 预训练、微调、对齐方法
5. **Robot platforms & data collection** — 硬件平台、遥操作数据采集
6. **Datasets, data augmentation, evaluation benchmarks** — 数据生态与评测
